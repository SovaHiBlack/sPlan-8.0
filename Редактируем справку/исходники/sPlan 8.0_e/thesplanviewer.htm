The "sPlan-Viewer"

 

The sPlan-Viewer is a free and independent program with which sPlan files can be opened, viewed and printed out. This gives every user access to sPlan files.


The sPlan-viewer is freeware. You are free to pass on the viewer and thus also give other users the opportunity to view or print out your drawings without sPlan having to be installed on the relevant computer.


The sPlan-Viewer is freely available here on our homepage:
