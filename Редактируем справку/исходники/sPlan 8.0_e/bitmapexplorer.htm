Bitmap-Explorer

Miscellaneous Functions ››


All bitmaps used in your project are listed in the Bitmap-Explorer. In this way you can quickly get an overview of all bitmaps, their resolution and their memory requirements.


You can open the Bitmap-Explorer at any time with the Bitmap-Explorer... command from the Functions menu. If you have selected a bitmap, you can also open the Bitmap-Explorer directly in the properties panel on the right-hand edge of the window:



Here you can directly specify a maximum value for the resolution (DPI) of the bitmaps and automatically adjust all bitmaps with a higher resolution to this maximum value. Depending on the bitmaps used, this can save a lot of memory and thus make your sPlan project more handy and faster.
